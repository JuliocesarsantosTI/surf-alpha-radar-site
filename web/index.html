Surf Alpha Radar

A local-first crypto research copilot built on Surf CLI. It turns raw Surf data into a transparent Alpha Score, a plain-language read, and a risk scan — all running on your own machine with your own Surf credits, at zero cost to anyone else.

How it works

Three pieces work together:

  1. This web app — the interface you're using. It sends your question to the local connector.
  2. Local Connector — a small app on your computer (127.0.0.1:8787) that runs Surf CLI commands, parses the results, computes scores, and caches them.
  3. Surf CLI — the data engine. Every request uses your Surf credits from your own machine, so nothing is routed through a shared server.

Instant vs Research

The two buttons on the home search box control how deep — and how many credits — a scan uses:

⚡ Instant

~3 credits · fast

  • Live price & 24h momentum
  • RSI technicals
  • Market Fear & Greed
  • Alpha Score + TL;DR

Best for a quick read.

🔬 Research

~6–9 credits · deeper

  • Everything in Instant, plus:
  • Social attention (smart followers + sentiment)
  • On-chain activity (EVM tokens)
  • ETF net flows, open interest, funding
  • Long/short ratio & latest news

Best for a serious decision.

You can also start with Instant and hit “Run full Research →” on the result to upgrade the same scan.

Reading a scan

  • Alpha Score (0–100) — a transparent gauge; each segment shows exactly which signal contributed. Inputs marked ~ are estimated (an endpoint wasn't run or isn't available for that token).
  • Signal Read — the “why”, in plain language, from the real data.
  • Risk Scan — liquidity, creator wallet, honeypot and holder-concentration checks.
  • Crypto Pulse — a market dashboard: live Fear & Greed, mindshare sentiment, and top mindshare leaders.

Asking free-form questions

Type a ticker (Analyze SOL, $PEPE) or a wallet address to get a full signal analysis. Ask a general question instead (“what is the best crypto dex?”) and the app runs a web search via Surf and shows the top results — so you're never stuck with an error. General questions use a small number of credits; token analysis is where the deep scoring lives.

Credit-safe by design

Opening the app spends zero credits. Nothing calls Surf until you ask for it:

  • Signal Rankings and Crypto Pulse load only when you click Load, then cache in your browser for 30 minutes — so revisiting them during the day is free.
  • Past chats reopen instantly from a local snapshot (0 credits). A ↻ Refresh link re-runs any scan live only when you want fresh data.
  • Instant scans stay light (~3 credits); Research is opt-in for the heavier signals.

Credits & privacy

Surf CLI gives 30 free credits per day per IP; heavier plans are available from Surf. Results are cached for a few minutes so repeated scans don't spend extra credits. Your API key and history stay on your machine — the connector binds to localhost only.
